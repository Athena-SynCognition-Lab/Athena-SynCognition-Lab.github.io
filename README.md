## 📖 About Us

**Athena SynCognition Lab** is an interdisciplinary research lab where we integrate **Natural Language Processing**, **Computer Vision**, **Psychology**, **Digital Health**, and **Computer-Aided Drug Design** using artificial intelligence to solve complex challenges.

### Our Mission
To develop affordable, effective, and accessible AI-driven solutions, proposed solutions, and algorithm design across:
- 🧠 Natural Language Processing
- 👁️ Computer Vision
- 🧬 Mental Health Assessment
- 🏥 Medical Image Analysis
- 💊 Protein Design & Drug Discovery
- 📱 Digital Health Platforms

Bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications.

---

## 🔬 Research Areas

| Area | Focus | Key Techniques |
|:-----|:------|:----------------|
| **Natural Language Processing** | Multilingual sentiment analysis, conversational AI, QA analysis, summarization, medical text for healthcare | Transformers, LLMs, Low-Resource NLP |
| **Computer Vision** | Medical image analysis (Classification, Segmentation, Object Detection), pattern recognition, edge detection, clinical settings | CNN, YOLO, Medical Imaging |
| **Psychology & Cognitive Science** | Human behavioral pattern analysis, cognitive load detection, mental health assessment tools | Behavioral Analysis, Mental Health, UX Research |
| **Human-Computer Interaction** | Digital twins for monitoring, health data analytics | Digital Twin, Health Informatics, AI in Healthcare |
| **Computer-Aided Drug Design** | Computational modeling and simulation to accelerate drug discovery, predict protein structures, optimize lead compounds | Molecular Docking, AI-Driven Drug Discovery, Protein Structure Prediction |
