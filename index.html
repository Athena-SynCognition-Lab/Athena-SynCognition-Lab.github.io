Who We Are

About Us

Athena SynCognition Lab is an interdisciplinary research lab where we integrate Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Psychology, Digital Health, and Computer-Aided Drug Design using artificial intelligence to solve complex challenges

Our mission is to develop affordable, effective, and accessible AI-driven solutions, proposed solutions, and algorithm design across NLP, computer vision, mental health assessment, medical image analysis, protein design, drug discovery, and digital health platforms bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world clinical applications.

Research Areas

Natural Language Processing

Multilingual sentiment analysis, conversational AI, QA analysis, summarization, and medical text for healthcare applications.

  • Transformers
  • LLMs
  • Low-Resource NLP

Computer Vision

Medical image analysis (Classification, Segmenttaion, Object Detection), pattern recognition, edge detection, and real-time object detection for clinical settings.

  • CNN
  • YOLO
  • Medical Imaging

Psychology & Cognitive Science

Human behavioral pattern analysis, cognitive load detection, and mental health assessment tools.

  • Behavioral Analysis
  • Mental Health
  • UX Research
  • Human Computer Interaction

Digital Health

Digital twins for monitoring, health data analytics

  • Digital Twin
  • Health Informatics
  • AI in Healthcare

Computer Aided Drug Design and Protein Design

Computational modeling and simulation to accelerate drug discovery, predict protein structures, and optimize lead compounds

  • Molecular Docking
  • AI-Driven Drug Discovery
  • Protein Structure Prediction
